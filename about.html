About Me

I'm Jacob, and I'm a husband, hunter, teacher, and avid dog lover! I have been teaching middle and high school math for the last 5 years, but recently I've felt a calling for something to change in my life. I wanted to find an environment where I could be inspired and challenged. That's the reason I've decided to better myself by becoming a web developer.


When I was in college, I never felt like I was particularly drawn to any major or career. I went without a plan unfortunately, and that was a struggle for me for some time. I decided to pursue teaching in the hopes that I could become a role model for the young men and women of tomorrow, but as the years pass by, I felt less passionate towards teaching. Through meticulous research and networking, I was moved to begin a journey learning web development as a way to challenge myself to jump out of rut that I had been going through with teaching.
